Support FAQ
What should I bring for a PC build appointment?

Bring all components, PSU cables, and your OS license key. Our engineer will verify compatibility before assembly.

Can I reschedule a service visit?

Yes — reschedule up to 24 hours before your slot via Contact Support or call our helpline.

Is on-site service available in my city?

On-site visits are available in Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Mumbai. Remote support is available nationwide.
